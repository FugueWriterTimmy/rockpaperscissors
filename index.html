Welcome to the RPS-25 Hub

RPS-25 is an expanded version of the classic game Rock-Paper-Scissors, featuring 25 different options instead of just three. Each choice can beat exactly 12 others, lose to 12, and draw with itself. This creates a complex web of interactions that makes every match more strategic, chaotic, and fun.
